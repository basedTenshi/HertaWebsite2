Herta (Puppet Form)

Relic Sets

  • Hunter of Glacial Forest (4-piece): Enhances CRIT DMG after using Ultimate, aligning with Herta's AoE capabilities.
  • Alternative: 2-piece combinations of Hunter of Glacial Forest and Musketeer of Wild Wheat for balanced ATK and Ice DMG bonuses.

Planar Ornaments

  • Rutilant Arena (2-piece): Boosts CRIT Rate and enhances follow-up attack damage.
  • Alternative: Inert Salsotto (2-piece) for increased CRIT Rate and Ultimate DMG.

Light Cones

  • Today Is Another Peaceful Day (4★): Provides a DMG boost based on energy cost, synergizing with Herta's Ultimate.
  • Geniuses' Repose (4★): Increases CRIT DMG after defeating an enemy, complementing Herta's AoE attacks.
  • Night on the Milky Way (5★): Increases ATK for each enemy on the field, ideal for multi-target scenarios.

Trace Priorities

  • Talent: Enhances follow-up attack damage.
  • Ultimate: Primary source of AoE damage.
  • Skill: Contributes to AoE damage and triggers follow-up attacks.
  • Basic ATK: Least priority; used when conserving skill points.

Stats Priorities

  • Body: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG.
  • Feet: ATK%.
  • Planar Sphere: Ice DMG Bonus.
  • Link Rope: ATK%.
  • Substats: CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Speed.

Team Composition

  • Main DPS: Herta (Puppet Form)
  • Support: Bronya (for ATK and CRIT buffs), Pela (for DEF debuffs)
  • Sustain: Bailu or Natasha (for healing)

The Herta (True Form)

Relic Sets

  • Scholar Lost in Erudition (4-piece): Maximizes damage output, especially for Enhanced Skills.
  • Hunter of Glacial Forest (4-piece): Provides CRIT DMG boost post-Ultimate, suitable for burst damage.

Planar Ornaments

  • Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm (2-piece): Enhances Ice DMG and overall damage potential.
  • Rutilant Arena (2-piece): Boosts CRIT Rate and follow-up attack damage.

Light Cones

  • Into the Unreachable Veil (5★): Signature Light Cone offering CRIT Rate and Skill DMG bonuses.
  • Today Is Another Peaceful Day (4★): Provides a DMG boost based on energy cost, synergizing with The Herta's Ultimate.
  • Geniuses' Repose (4★): Increases CRIT DMG after defeating an enemy, complementing AoE attacks.

Trace Priorities

  • Enhanced Skill: Primary damage source, scales with "Interpretation" stacks.
  • Ultimate: Provides ATK buff and immediate action, crucial for damage rotation.
  • Talent: Facilitates "Interpretation" stack application, enhancing overall damage.
  • Basic ATK: Used when conserving skill points; lowest priority.

Stats Priorities

  • Body: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG.
  • Feet: ATK% / Speed
  • Planar Sphere: Ice DMG Bonus.
  • Link Rope: ATK%.
  • Substats: CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Speed.

Team Composition

  • Main DPS: The Herta (True Form)
  • Sub-DPS: Jade or Argenti (to apply additional "Interpretation" stacks)
  • Support: Robin (for team-wide buffs), Pela (for DEF debuffs)
  • Sustain: Lingsha or Gallagher (for healing and SP management)
